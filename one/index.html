Seattle Summer Night

This picture is from a Seattle summer night, near the end of the season during one of the final heat waves. It was really hot, so my friends and I went swimming at a dock on Lake Washington. We ran into a friend who has a boat, and he offered to take us out. It all lined up perfectly—it was a spontaneous, fun night. Everyone was in the moment; no one was on their phones, though I did snap a picture. We were just enjoying each other’s company. Looking back, I thought it was a precious memory at the time, but now it evokes nostalgia and warm feelings. We’re all living separate lives across the country post-college, and it makes me appreciate how, in college, you’re surrounded by your friends within a small radius—a closeness that rarely happens in adult life. This picture evokes strong feelings of friendship, nostalgia, and gratitude, as I’m still friends with these people.
