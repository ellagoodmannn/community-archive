Steamed

This photo was taken after a very fulfilling, fun day with friends. I got home and I was so tired and had just driven for like two hours. I was really excited to eat steamed broccoli and carrots, because it's not something I've made here on the East Coast, but it's what I grew up eating. It's what my Dad cooked, and it felt very much like a childhood memory. Literally just microwaved broccoli and carrots with pepper and salt. So much joy in that photo.
