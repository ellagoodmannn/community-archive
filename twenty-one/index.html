New Home, Tall Plant

Okay, hopefully I'm doing this right. This is the first week I moved to New York. I had touched down two days ago and was trying to figure out how I could stay emotionally regulated in the city. I'm so used to having green space, park space, and quiet spaces to sit, and I felt like I wasn't able to get any place quiet where I could just think, recuperate, and have a thought to myself. I went on a walk, went to Central Park, and found this really tall plant. I think it put a lot of things into perspective. I was used to feeling like, obviously not the biggest thing in the city because there are so many buildings, but then seeing unexpected things growing really tall as well made me feel centered and grounded, like a little slice of home. So yeah, this was maybe the beginning of August, and I felt reflective and excited.
